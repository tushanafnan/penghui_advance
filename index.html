Our Advantage

Why Choose Penghui China Wood

Certified Quality

All timber holds FSC® Chain of Custody certification. We maintain full traceability from forest to your facility, meeting EU Timber Regulation (EUTR) requirements.

Own Manufacturing

Our 12,000 m² factory in Tallinn, Estonia houses 8 kilns and precision CNC milling. No middlemen — you buy direct from producer.

Fast Turnaround

Standard orders ship within 5 business days. Stocked items within 48 hours. Custom-dimensioned orders processed within 10–14 days.

Pan-European Logistics

Regular groupage routes to Germany, Netherlands, France, Italy and Scandinavia. Container loads and LTL available. Incoterms: EXW, FOB, CFR, DDP.

Dedicated Support

Your account manager speaks your language — we have native speakers for DE, FR, IT, NL, PL and EN. WhatsApp and video call available.

Custom Solutions

Bespoke dimensions, species blends, surface treatments and packaging. Our team works with your specifications — not the other way around.

Our Story

About Penghui China Wood

Two decades of expertise in Baltic hardwoods, serving European markets with integrity and precision.

Since 2004

Built on Trust,
Grown in the Forest

Penghui China Wood was founded in Tallinn in 2004 by timber engineer Andrus Kask, who saw an opportunity to connect Estonia's exceptional forest resources with Europe's growing demand for quality hardwoods.

From a single sawmill and a handful of trusted foresters, we've grown into a full-service wood export company with our own 12,000 m² manufacturing facility, 8 industrial drying kilns, and a team of 140 specialists.

Today, Penghui China Wood serves over 280 business partners in 30 countries, from boutique furniture makers in Copenhagen to large construction companies in Bavaria. Our principle has never changed: every plank we sell carries our name.
